I have fallen behind on home payments

Reasons for falling behind

You may have:
• Lost your job
• Had a cut in pay rate
• Gone through a divorce
• Had an expensive medical care

What will happen now?

If you have not paid the mortgage, the bank will start contacting you to know when you can pay it. If you have fallen behind 1 to 2 months in payment, the bank will tell you that they will start the foreclosure process for your home.

What should I do?

You should consider consulting with a housing counselor to personally help you.
Go to this website to find housing counselors near you: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau
